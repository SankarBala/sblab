11 Best Bootstrap Portfolio Templates 2025

With these predefined Bootstrap portfolio templates, you can start working on your web presence immediately.

We studied many available solutions on the market and found that not all are as reliable as they claim to be.

But that’s not something to worry about with this collection of good web designs.

You can be a freelancer, photographer, programmer, designer, or whatever – these templates will make an immediate difference.

They use only the latest technologies and ensure exceptional performance across all devices and platforms.

Skip the process of starting from the ground up, when you can enjoy a quick start with a ready-to-use template.

There’s no right or wrong choice. It’s more personal preference, but we like the first three the most!

Best Bootstrap Portfolio Templates

1. Portfolio 2


Portfolio 2 is the second edition of Colorlib’s popular template for building powerful online portfolio websites. It offers a quick solution for setting up an online presence that will impress and inspire your visitors.

Instead of starting from scratch, you can now save heaps of time. If you stick to the default configuration, you will enjoy a speedy website execution.

Just light design tweaks and you are all set to rock and roll.

Portfolio 2 is also equipped with the latest technologies to guarantee a performance that will take your site experience to the next level.

Download Preview

2. Glint


Glint is another of our favorite Bootstrap portfolio template that makes an immediate difference. Why even go through the hassle of starting from square one if you don’t have to?

You will achieve just as amazing a result with a ready-to-use site canvas!

And Glint is excellent proof of that.

You can preview the live demo first, which will give you a better understanding of how awesome Glint istoo awesome!

Some features you get are the parallax effect, on-scroll content loading, a back-to-top button, and animated statistics. This is just a small segment of all the goodies that make Glint oh special.

Download Preview

3. Jackco


Jackco is a bright, vibrant, modern Bootstrap website template for portfolios of all kinds. With it, you can promote yourself to the world in the best possible light, no matter what you specialize in.

Jackco is here for all of you! The code structure is also beginner-friendly, so you can quickly and comfortably get the most out of Jackco.

What’s cool about Jackco is its relatively distinct look. If you want to stand out from the masses, consider Jackco. It comes with multiple layouts, forms, timelines, and other useful sections that take things to the next level.

Download Preview

4. Oraxol


For everyone with a passion for dark and creative design, that’s when Oraxol comes into play. This Bootstrap portfolio template works well, but it also gives you complete freedom if you’d like to work on design improvements and other whatnot.

Do what you want – Oraxol will handle it with ease!

Its layout adjusts to every screen size smoothly, so you don’t lose on quality experience browsing on mobile.

Presenting your services and portfolio and even starting a blog has never been as fun as it is with Oraxol. A functional contact form is also available, so you don’t need to create it from scratch.

Download Preview

5. Space


Space takes things one step further regarding simplicity and minimalism. If you are after this, your best bet is simply going with Space.

This Bootstrap portfolio template adapts to any creative mind out there. Not just that, it will boost your workflow through the roof, while still maintaining your professionalism intact.

The main highlight of Space is the awesome content reveal effect that spices things up without overwhelming and distracting the user.

If you want to build a one-page portfolio website, go with Space again. A bombing template that will wow everyone.

Download Preview

6. Photosen


Photosen is a more exclusive Bootstrap portfolio template aimed at amateur and pro photographers by default. Of course, if you dig the style and dark mode, you can easily tailor it to other professions and services.

It has this awesome grid-style home page that you can use for different categories, services, or photography styles.

Keep in mind, everything is customizable, so you can introduce your creative touch without a sweat. Other features include a multi-level drop-down menu, social media icons, hover effects, contact and about pages.

Differentiate from the masses with confidence. Pick Photosen.

Download Preview

7. Po-Portfolio


If you are primarily looking for designs that are not too traditional, Po-Portfolio is another one to peek at. It’s a Bootstrap portfolio website template that instantly piques everyone’s interest.

You don’t need to edit it much, rather use it out of the box and save yourself even more time and energy. Add your personal touch, but that will happen in little to no time.

Grid layout with hove effect, scroll down button, sidebar menu, creative blog section and compatibility with devices and browsers are (not) all the features you get in the Po-Portfolio kit. Enjoy.

Download Preview

8. Unfold


Unfold your real potential with Unfold, a powerful, remarkable and eye-catchy Bootstrap portfolio template.

You cannot go wrong with this one if you are a black/dark design fan. It’s special to the very last detail. Plus, it features a single-page layout with a neat sticky menu (only appears when you start scrolling back to the top).

The bundle of features contains parallax effect, content reveal, brand logo slider and other neat animations and components.

I also like the footer reveal, which is uncommon on portfolio websites. Heck, pages in general.

Treat yourself right.

Download Preview

9. Calvin


Designers may showcase their portfolio best with Calvin, but this Bootstrap portfolio template offers a plethora of other options and possibilities.

In other words, there is no need to limit yourself in any way, shape, or form. With Calvin, you can create your dream online presence quickly.

You’ll still have a blast using it, even as a newbie. Calvin is perfect for someone building their first portfolio page or someone who is an expert.

Moreover, since it’s based on Bootstrap, you know the layout is 100% fluid, working smoothly on all screen sizes.

Make an impact with Calvin.

Download Preview

10. Rezume


With Rezume, creating CV and resume websites is easy. Let me mention first that you will do yourself a favor no matter which template you pick from this list.

Rezume is no different.

A powerful Bootstrap-based template for creatives and job seekers. Instead of doing what everyone else does, do it your way with Rezume.

Promote your creative spirit and professional services like a champ. With Rezume, duh!

When building such a website, don’t forget to include client testimonials to help build trust. That’s something you can make happen by default with Rezume.

But there are so many other highlights that I’ll let you experience first-hand by visiting the live demo preview.

Download Preview

11. Monarchy


Minimalism and simplicity always win. And if you blend them with some artistry, that’s even better. But you don’t have to because Monarchy already did all the work instead of you.

How cool.

You get different front and internal page layouts to showcase your business online expertly. You can use each layout without changing much or customize it further.

You can do you!

Furthermore, you can start a blog, which can work as a medium to get on an even more personal level with your (potential) clients and content marketing.

Achieve great things with Monarchy without breaking a single drop of sweat.
